Project Idea

Voice-Assisted Emergency Room Finder

Inspired by ProPublica’s "ER Wait Watcher" project, I used average wait time data from the Center for Medicare and Medicaid Services and real-time traffic data from Google Maps. By adding the average wait time at hospitals to real-time traffic data, the program will estimate the hospital where the patient can be seen fastest.

Using this data, I created a Google Home action that users can ask what hospital will likely see you fastest, and Google will return a response.
